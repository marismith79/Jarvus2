Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Jarvus. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name and email address
  • Authentication information
  • Usage data and preferences
  • OAuth credentials for connected services (Gmail, Calendar, etc.)

2.2 Usage Data

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device information
  • Error logs and performance data

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Process your authentication and authorization
  • Connect and manage your integrated services
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data. This includes:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure storage of OAuth credentials

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with various third-party services (Google, Microsoft, etc.). When you connect these services:

  • We only access the data you explicitly authorize
  • We store credentials securely
  • You can revoke access at any time
  • We comply with each service's terms of use

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
privacy@jarvus.com

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
